About Company Page


We provide a distributed, secure, and private data exchange to enable end-to-end, client-first case management amongst social service provider .Sakhi educates and empowers women who help us lead our daily lives comfortably.We try to contribute by providing a platform where they can seek professional help and also reach out to the organisations that look forward to creating an impact.



Our Values


MISSION : Through service design, empower social service providers to solve, not merely manage, problems their clients face.

VISION : Everyone, no matter how wanting of circumstance, has access to the right help at the right moment one needs it.
